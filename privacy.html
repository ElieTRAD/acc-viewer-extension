Privacy Policy – ACC Viewer Extension

ACC Viewer Extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

The extension only modifies the Autodesk Construction Cloud Web Viewer interface to expose a hidden toolbar button and categorize Revit model elements.

This action occurs when the extension is clicked and is effective only per session; if the page is refreshed, the extension must be clicked again.

No data is transmitted to any server, and no personally identifiable information is accessed.
